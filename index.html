About Me


Hi. My name is Qingbo. I began my journey into computer science during my second year of highschool. I decided that it would be fun to take a computer science course at the time because I really enjoyed working with technology. Before discovering programming, I used to love (mindlessly) editing videos and posting some of them on youtube. If you want to check out my youtube channel: runescapegame. However, that changed because the programming experience was so incredible, especially the fact that I was able to create something from nothing by myself with code alone. I knew at that point that computer science is going to be the path I want to take in my life.

I love challenges and I love learning new things. Everytime I set myself out to complete a task, I feel like I am learning something new. Whether it is creating video editing or coding or completely something else, there is so much that I still do not know and I strive to improve each and everyday. Everytime I finish a project, I feel a huge sense of accomplishment. The more challenging a project is, the greater the satisfaction at the end. It almost feels like I was brought into this world to code and create new things.

My goal in life is to one day make an impact in the world. I do not wish to pass on with my name forgotten. I want to create an app that would make life easier for people and truly redefine how we live lives, and I would like to see myself doing just that in 5-10 years from now.

My Academic Projects

Some of my academic projects from the last semester at Ryerson.

Library Watchdog


The library watchdog was created to keep the library quiet. It will constantly scan the surrounding area for the loudest noise and move towards it. Once it finds the noise source, it will tell the noisemaker to quiet down. It is equipped with the latest technology which allows it to avoid falling off platforms, and avoid obstacles. This has many other utilizations such being used as a scout to find trapped survivors during an earthquake.

Click here to check it out in action!

Weather App


This weather app allows you to check weather in cities from all over the world! Just enter the city name and watch it fetch all the data. Data is parsed from openweathermap.org in JSON format. Comes with 7 days long term forcast and 3-hours shorterm forcast.
